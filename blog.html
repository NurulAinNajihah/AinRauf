Mochi, My Best Friend

Mochi was one of the nicest cat and best friend that I will ever had. He may have been with me only for a short time but my love for him stays forever. The moment I saw his tummy started to be abnormally large...I knew something was up. I bought him to the vet and did an x-ray to him, turns out he had a coronavirus inside him called Feline Infectious Peritonitis (FIP) that causing inflamentation. And not long after discovering it, he had gone to a better place but at least I got to hold him close on the last minutes with him in this world
